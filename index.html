Our Love Story

It all started on July 20th, 2024, when I randomly messaged her "Hi" on Instagram. To my surprise, she replied within hours. I was shocked, excited, and completely nervous. She was my crush, someone I had noticed before but never had the courage to approach. I first saw her sister, who looked quite similar, at my cousin's wedding. But later, I found out she had a younger sister, the one who had captured my heart.

We had crossed paths on a religious trip, and though I noticed her, I never dared to talk to her. And now, here she was, replying to my message. It took me two whole days to gather the courage to respond again. When I asked how she was, she casually replied, "Fine, what about you?" I nervously said, "Mast," and our conversation slowly began.

As we kept chatting day and night, we got to know each other better. Both of us were from Bhavnagar, Gujarat, from the same caste, and even lived in the same area! Coincidentally, she was moving to a new address – right opposite my home where my parents lived.

Eventually, I convinced her to switch to iMessage, and that’s when she gave me her number. Three days later, out of the blue, she called me while I was busy with household chores. That first call lasted two hours, and from there, our chats grew longer into the night.

We've shared so many beautiful moments since then. Recently, we went on a pizza date, but her elder sister spotted us, and now, I'm waiting for her to talk to their family about us.
